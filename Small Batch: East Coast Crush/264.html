Small Batch: East Coast Crush Session Vermont IPA.

East Coast Crush is a Vermont style pale ale, packaged and served using our custom LIVE beer dispense. A session Vermont IPA, with low background bitterness, loaded with intense juicy mango, pineapple, peach and pine.
ABV: 4.8%
Food pairing: Chickpea and Apricot Tagine, Pan-fried Lemon Sole, Lobster Salad
