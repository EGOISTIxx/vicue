Motueka Single Hop India Pale Ale – 2012.

Lemon and lime rise to the top of this hoppy brew, with supporting acts of tropical fruit for a very rounded, thoroughly satisfying flavour. Motueka is a hop we've used in loads of recipes; it has enough resin to balance the intense fruity character.
ABV: 6.7%
Food pairing: Tofu pad Thai with fresh lime juice and coriander, Fish tacos with avocado, Key lime pie
