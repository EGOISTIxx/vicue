Bitch Please (w/ 3 Floyds) Barley Wine - 3 Floyds Collab.

This limited edition American Barley Wine was brewed in collaboration with 3 Floyds Brewery. This beer had all the warm, boozy and smoky aspects of an Islay Scotch whisky with the sweet malt and devastatingly bitter attributes of a barley wine. Peat smoke features prominently, backed up with a complex fruity hop profile.
ABV: 11.5%
Food pairing: Stilton Blue featured cheese platter, Dark bitter chocolate torte, Scotch bonnet beef chilli
