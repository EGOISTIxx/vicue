All Day Long - Prototype Challenge Malty Low ABV Ale.

Prototype Challenge 2014. All Day Long is a mild like no other. This complex, low-alcohol brew was the third of our 2014 Prototypes. Featuring a boatload of seven different speciality malts and some of our favourite hops, this beer is resinous at first, transitioning into something akin to bitter orange marmalade spread onto a thick bread crust. This is for everyone who said BrewDog would never brew a mild...
ABV: 2.8%
Food pairing: Chunky orange marmalade on toast, Prawn toast with sweet chilli sauce, Lemon tart
