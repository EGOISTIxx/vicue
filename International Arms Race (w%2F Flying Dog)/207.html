International Arms Race (w/ Flying Dog) Zero IBU Herbal IPA.

The International Arms Race was a new type of battle collaboration between hardcore American craft brewery Flying Dog and BrewDog. Both breweries attempted to brew an IPA with absolutely no hops.
ABV: 7.5%
Food pairing: Rosemary and thyme grilled chicken, Light vegetable and herb soup with toasted rye bread, Earl Grey ice cream with a lavender sauce
